### Brief Description of Usage: clarifying through an email to the teachers about the exact tasks our robot is performing and problems that it is trying to resolve.

We have designed our robot for the following scenario:
- Environment: A STEM lab / activity room in a kindergarten
- Target Users: Children

Key features of our robot include:
- Identifying children who raise their hands while patrolling in the room, and navigating to them to offer assistance (dispensing extra building blocks, seeking teacher's help when necessary).
- Detecting the sound of crying and alerting the teacher to potential incidents.
